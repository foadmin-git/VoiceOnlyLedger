Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2025

Summary: VoiceOnlyLedger does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All your expense records are stored locally on your device or optionally synced through your personal iCloud account.

Data Collection

VoiceOnlyLedger does not collect any personal information. We do not have servers, databases, or any infrastructure to store your data.

Data Storage

All your expense records are stored:

  • Locally: On your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • iCloud (Optional): If you enable iCloud sync, your data is synced through your personal iCloud account

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Microphone: Used solely for speech recognition to convert your voice into text for recording expenses
  • Speech Recognition: Uses Apple's on-device speech recognition to process your voice input

Third-Party Services

VoiceOnlyLedger does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • View all your data within the app
  • Delete individual records
  • Clear all data using the "Clear All Records" feature in Settings
  • Disable iCloud sync at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store page.
