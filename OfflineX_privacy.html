Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

TL;DR: OfflineX is a privacy-first app. We collect zero data. Everything stays on your device. No internet connection required, no data transmitted, period.

1. Information We Collect

We collect absolutely no personal information.

OfflineX is designed to work entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Device information
  • Crash logs or diagnostics

2. Data Storage

All data created within OfflineX is stored locally on your device only:

  • Text notes are saved to your device's local storage
  • Generated QR codes are saved to your photo library (only when you explicitly choose to save)
  • Calculator history and settings are stored locally

We have no access to this data. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.

3. Permissions

OfflineX requests the following permissions solely for functionality:

  • Camera: Only for scanning QR codes. Camera data is processed in real-time and never stored or transmitted.
  • Photo Library: Only to save generated QR codes or select images for QR scanning. We access only the images you select.
  • Motion Sensors: Only for the level tool functionality. Motion data is processed in real-time and never stored.
  • Location: Only for the compass to determine magnetic north. Location data is processed in real-time and never stored or transmitted.

4. Third-Party Services

OfflineX does not use any third-party services.

We do not integrate with:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media SDKs
  • Crash reporting services
  • Cloud storage services

5. Internet Connection

OfflineX is designed to work without any internet connection. The app does not:

  • Require internet to function
  • Send any data over the network
  • Connect to remote servers
  • Download or upload any content

6. Children's Privacy

OfflineX does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

Email: support@offlinex.app

Our Commitment: Your privacy is our top priority. OfflineX was built from the ground up to respect your privacy. No tracking, no data collection, no compromises.
